This contract outlines the terms for a series of urban exploration missions focused on locating and documenting lost items, treasures, and artifacts from U.S. historical events. All activities comply with federal, state, and local laws, including the Archaeological Resources Protection Act (ARPA) and National Historic Preservation Act (NHPA). Mission Objectives

The primary goal is to investigate and potentially resolve unsolved historical mysteries involving lost treasures across the United States. Sponsors seek high-quality documentation for public dissemination (documentaries, publications). Participants conduct non-destructive surveys and recovery attempts using advanced equipment.
